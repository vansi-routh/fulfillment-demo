Privacy Policy

Spectrum HH — Store Associate Application

Enterprise / MDM-Managed Device Application
Effective Date: March 16, 2025 Last Updated: March 16, 2025 Application: Spectrum HH (com.peapod.nextgen.shop) Platform: Android

📋 1. Overview

Spectrum HH (hereinafter "the Application") is an enterprise-grade, line-of-business Android application developed and operated by Royal Ahold Delhaize and its affiliated brands (collectively "the Company", "we", "us", or "our"). The Application is distributed exclusively to store associates employed by or contracted with the Company.

The Application is deployed exclusively on company-owned devices that are enrolled in and fully managed by a corporate Mobile Device Management (MDM) solution — such as Microsoft Intune or VMware Workspace ONE (AirWatch). It is not available to the general public and is not intended for installation on personal devices.

This Privacy Policy explains which device capabilities and data the Application accesses, why those capabilities are necessary, and how related information is handled. Because the Application operates in a fully controlled, enterprise-managed environment, all data collection and processing is performed solely to fulfill legitimate business operations.

👥 2. Scope & Intended Audience

This policy applies to all users of the Application — i.e., current store associates and authorised Company personnel who are issued a managed device pre-loaded with the Application. End-consumers and members of the public are not users of this Application.

Because every device is centrally managed by the Company's IT department, individual users do not install, configure, or independently consent to app permissions. All permissions are reviewed, approved, and enforced at the organisational level through the MDM platform's compliance policies.

🔐 4. Device Permissions & Justifications

The Application declares the following Android permissions in its manifest. Each permission is necessary for a specific, documented business function. No permission is used to collect or monetise personal data.

Permission Category Business Justification Required?
BLUETOOTH Bluetooth Enables the Application to connect with Bluetooth-enabled enterprise hardware peripherals (e.g. Zebra barcode scanners, receipt printers, label printers) assigned to store associates. Classic Bluetooth pairing and communication with already-bonded devices requires this permission. Required
BLUETOOTH_ADMIN Bluetooth Required to initiate Bluetooth device discovery and manage the paired-device list on the managed device. This ensures store associates can pair scanners and peripherals without needing to leave the Application. Required
BLUETOOTH_CONNECT Bluetooth Introduced in Android 12 (API 31). Required to connect to previously-paired Bluetooth devices at runtime. Without this permission on Android 12+, the Application cannot communicate with bonded Zebra peripherals. Required
INTERNET Network Core permission that allows the Application to communicate with Company back-end APIs (order management, inventory services, pick-and-pack services, authentication). All traffic is routed over HTTPS to Company-controlled endpoints only. Required
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Network Allows the Application to check whether a network connection is available before attempting API calls, providing appropriate offline-mode messaging to store associates and preventing unnecessary errors. Required
ACCESS_WIFI_STATE Network Enables the Application to determine Wi-Fi connection status and signal quality. Store operations (particularly large file transfers such as planograms and stock images) are optimised for Wi-Fi; this permission allows the Application to select the appropriate data transfer strategy. Required
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Storage Allows the Application to write operational data files (e.g. pick lists, planogram images, log files) to the device's external storage. Used for offline caching and report generation on devices running Android 9 and below. Required
MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Storage On Android 11+ this all-files-access permission is required by the Zebra EMDK and other hardware integration libraries to read and write configuration files and firmware update packages to locations outside the Application's sandboxed storage. The Company's MDM policy grants this permission at the device management layer. Required
CAMERA Camera Used to scan barcodes and QR codes via the device's built-in camera. This is a core store-operations function (e.g. product identification, tote scanning, shelf verification). The camera is only activated when the associate explicitly triggers a scan action within the Application; no images or video are stored, transmitted, or shared. Required
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Notifications Required on Android 13+ (API 33) to display push notifications. The Application uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver real-time operational alerts to store associates — e.g. order arrival notifications, task reminders, and system status messages from store management. Required
FOREGROUND_SERVICE Background Processing Allows the Application to run a foreground service, which keeps critical data-sync operations visible and active while store associates temporarily switch to another app. Required
FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC Background Processing Categorises the foreground service specifically as a "data sync" service type (required since Android 14). This enables the Application to synchronise order updates, inventory changes, and task queues in the background without interrupting associate workflows. Required
SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW System Overlay Enables the Application to display high-priority operational alerts (e.g. urgent order notifications, MDM compliance warnings) as system-level overlays, ensuring store associates are notified even when the Application is in the background. On MDM-managed devices this permission is granted by policy. Required
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM System Required to schedule time-critical tasks at exact intervals — for example, triggering an order pick-by deadline alert or a periodic data-consistency check at a precise time. This ensures business processes remain on schedule. Required
WAKE_LOCK System Prevents the device CPU from sleeping during active Bluetooth scanner communication and during foreground data-sync operations. Without this permission, ongoing hardware interactions may be interrupted by the device entering sleep mode mid-operation. Required
com.zebra.provider.READ Enterprise Hardware Zebra Technologies vendor permission. Grants read access to the Zebra Content Provider, which supplies device configuration, scanner profiles, and firmware information needed for integration with Zebra mobile computing devices (e.g. TC-series handhelds used by store associates). Required
com.symbol.emdk.permission.EMDK Enterprise Hardware Required to use the Zebra Enterprise Mobility Developer Kit (EMDK) APIs. EMDK provides advanced barcode scanning profiles, serial port access, and hardware key mapping for Zebra/Symbol devices. Without this permission the Application cannot use hardware scan triggers or advanced scanner configuration. Required

Note regarding optional hardware: The android.hardware.camera feature is declared with android:required="false", meaning the Application can be installed on devices without a camera. Camera functionality is gracefully degraded on such devices.

📊 5. Data Collected and How It Is Used

The Application collects only the minimum data required to perform store-operations functions. The following categories of data may be processed:

Data Category Purpose Retention
Employee ID / Username Authentication & audit trail for store operations Retained for the duration of employment; deleted on offboarding.
Device Identifiers MDM device enrolment, push notification routing (FCM token) Retained while the device is enrolled; removed on device wipe.
Operational Activity Logs Troubleshooting, audit compliance, performance monitoring Up to 90 days on Company servers; purged automatically.
Barcode / QR Scan Data Product identification, order processing, tote verification Transmitted to back-end in real time; not stored locally beyond the session.
Network Status Metadata Adaptive data-sync strategy selection Not stored; used transiently in memory only.
Push Notification Content Real-time operational alerts from store management systems Displayed to associate; not persisted beyond device notification tray.

The Application does not collect, access, or transmit: personal photos, contacts, call logs, location data, browsing history, advertising identifiers, or any other data unrelated to store operations.

🔗 6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Data processed by the Application is shared only with the following parties and only to the extent necessary:

  • Royal Ahold Delhaize Group companies and affiliated banners — for operational purposes (order management, inventory, workforce planning).
  • Google Firebase (Firebase Cloud Messaging) — solely for the delivery of push notifications. FCM tokens are used as routing addresses; message content originates from Company servers. Google's FCM service is governed by Google's Firebase Privacy and Security documentation.
  • Zebra Technologies — device hardware integration libraries (EMDK) are embedded in the Application. No personal data is transmitted to Zebra servers by these libraries.
  • MDM Provider (Microsoft Intune / VMware AirWatch) — the Company's MDM provider manages device enrolment, policy enforcement, and Application deployment. The MDM provider may process device metadata as described in its own privacy documentation.

We do not sell, rent, or share data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for commercial purposes.

🛡️ 7. Data Security

All data transmitted by the Application is protected using TLS 1.2 / TLS 1.3 encryption. Data at rest on the device is protected by the Android Keystore system and the device's full-disk or file-based encryption, which is enforced by MDM policy.

Application back-up is explicitly disabled (android:allowBackup="false") to prevent Application data from being included in Android cloud backups. This ensures sensitive operational data remains within Company-controlled infrastructure at all times.

Access to Company systems through the Application requires valid employee credentials managed by the Company's Identity & Access Management (IAM) system. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) may be enforced by the MDM conditional access policy.

📱 8. Managed Device Context

All devices running the Application are corporate-owned and fully managed under an MDM solution (Microsoft Intune or VMware Workspace ONE / AirWatch). This means:

  • Device enrolment, compliance checking, and policy enforcement are handled by the Company's IT department.
  • All Android permissions declared in the Application manifest are pre-approved and granted at the organisational level through the MDM platform's managed configuration; store associates are not required to individually accept or manage permissions.
  • Devices are subject to remote wipe capabilities, ensuring that data can be securely removed if a device is lost or an employee leaves the Company.
  • Application deployment and updates are pushed centrally by the IT department; associates do not have access to the Google Play Store for independent installation.
